Brief Introduction to HEMA(Historical European Martial Arts)

HEMA is a form of martial arts that encompasses dozens of different styles of combat from Italian, German, British, French, Spanish, Scottish and Portuguese schools that were developed between the 14th and 19th century. The styles of martial arts include both hand-to-hand/wrestling as well as techniques involving swords, daggers, spears, long knives and axes. Today there are several HEMA federations that host championships yearly for this martial art. To learn more about the history of HEMA you can read this Wikipedia article.
